About YUTRITION

YUTRITION is a website/evolving app that allows people stay healthy with meal plans (strictly to their physicals, goal, ), that provide a direct description of what you’d be consuming and tracks your calories throught the day. Our company knew there weren’t very many apps/websites in this market because food planning and healthy food choices are overlooked in the US, especially in black communities and low income neighborhoods. So, we wanted to be the start to helping by taking the first step towards a healthier lifestyle
